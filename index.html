About

Mondairz Builders — Engineers & Contractors

Quality Construction – On Time, Every Time

Mondairz Builders is a trusted name in residential, commercial, and turnkey construction across Mohali, Chandigarh, and Punjab. Led by Er. Accharpreet Singh Mondair, a Civil Engineer with over 16 years of experience, we deliver projects that blend strong engineering, modern design, and premium quality materials.

From house construction and commercial buildings to renovation, interiors, waterproofing, and landscaping, our team ensures transparent pricing, on-time delivery, and strict safety standards. Most of our clients come back to us for new projects or refer us to their friends and family — a testimony to our workmanship and commitment.
